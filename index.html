About me

Hi, I’m Vishwanatha Perala
I’m a London-based machine-learning engineer who likes shipping ideas, not just prototypes. Right now I’m rebuilding 55 years of missing weather data with computer-vision in-painting and letting an LLM-driven RAG pipeline draft the daily forecasts. Before that I was leading the AI/ML Centre of Excellence at OSI Digital, guiding a team of eight and rolling out LangChain-powered tools.

What I’m good at

  • Comfortable working with Python, PyTorch, FastAPI, LangChain, and Docker. From quick experiments to deployable ML systems.
  • Practical approach to MLOps with reproducible training runs, simple REST APIs, and GitHub Actions for clean deployments.
  • Enjoy translating research papers into working code, focused on getting things to run reliably on real hardware and timelines.
