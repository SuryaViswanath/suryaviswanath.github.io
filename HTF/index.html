About the HTF - You Even

Tired of seeing AI projects that are just wrappers on wrappers? Same. HTF – You Even? is a raw, weekly breakdown of real-world AI and ML problems. This series explores what it actually takes to build, debug, and deploy models in production – not just toy demos or tutorials.

Each post walks through a specific challenge you’re likely to face in the wild – like fine-tuning models with barely any data, deploying LLMs under strict latency budgets, or debugging hallucinations in RAG pipelines. We’re not here for fluff – we’re here to answer: How the F*ck do you do that?

Follow along if you're an engineer, researcher, or founder tired of the noise and looking for gritty, tactical insight. No hype. No magic. Just hard-earned lessons from the real world.
