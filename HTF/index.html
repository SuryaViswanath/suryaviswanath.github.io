About the HTF Series

The HTF Series is a collection of articles, tutorials, and insights dedicated to exploring cutting-edge techniques and concepts in the field of machine learning, privacy-preserving AI, and data science.

Each entry in this series dives deep into specific topics, providing both theoretical foundations and practical implementations to help researchers and practitioners advance their understanding and skills.

Whether you're interested in the mathematics behind differential privacy, the latest advancements in language model inference, or practical techniques for building robust machine learning pipelines, the HTF Series has something valuable to offer.
